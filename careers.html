On-time delivery of committed tasks. Good representation of the company with clients to keep them retained. Work with your team as one. Keep your code and our company in good shape.

Recommend and execute improvements
Create technical documentation for reference and reporting

Proven experience as a Back end Developer, Software Engineer or similar role
Familiarity with Agile development methodologies
Experience with software design and development in a test-driven environment

Excellent communication skills
Resourcefulness and troubleshooting aptitude
