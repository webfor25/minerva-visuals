Segment

Segment is a Customer Data Platform (CDP) that simplifies collecting and using first-party customer data. This data is collected in one place across all of your customer touchpoints. 
‍
Do you want to deliver a personalized experience to each customer?
First, you need to get to know your customers and understand what their needs and preferences are and how they interact with your company.
‍
Segment will provide you with this data and according to that, you can decide what is the best way is to approach them. This is the foundation for personalization - to reach your customers through the channel they prefer with the content they are most likely interested in.
‍
Segment allows you to collect, clean, control, and analyze data about your customers. Monitoring customers’ behavior, identifying trends, and analyzing how your product is being used helps you deliver a personalized user experience, track performance, boost your productivity and decision making. This platform can also enrich the customer data by connecting and aggregating them with your other tools. 
‍
More than 20,000 companies like Levi’s, FOX, Atlassian, Instacart, IBM, and Domino’s use Segment.
