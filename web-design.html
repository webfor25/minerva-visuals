Frequently asked Questions

How long will it take to complete my site?

V

Our standard time schedule for completing a website is 3 to 6 weeks, unless it is an e-shop, but it depends on the project.

The length and pace of each project is determined by each client. How much input you can provide in the initial stages, feedback and speed of content delivery - all affect the speed of completion. The required functionality and revisions will also play a role in delivery time.

Do I have to be in Europe to work together?

V

Nope! We work with clients from all over the world, we can communicate via e-mail, slack, scheduled calls or on Google Meet.

Will I be able to update the site myself when it's done?

V

Yes! We are happy when our clients take control of their website and learn how to manage content through our simple and pleasant control panel.

After your website completion, you will have full access to the control panel, where you can easily perform simple text updates, photo changes, add new products, change prices. add more team members or new partners. However, we recommend that one of our professional (developers) complete more complex changes and maintenance, such as design or structure changes and complex functionality requirements.

What if I don't like the look of my new website?

V

With each website, we have two goals:

1. User-Centric: Create a UI (User interface) that looks visually appealing with a (user-centric) UX (User experience) that is useful, useable and provide a clear (user-journey) to your visitors.

2. Conversion: Create a site that is prepared to convert visitors to potential customers, a website that is ready to implement a sophisticated marketing-automation tools that generate revenue and profit.

Will my site be suitable for mobile devices?

V

Absolutely! Having a responsive website on mobile is more important than ever! We work hard to make your site responsive to all modern devices and browsers.

Do I need to contribute a lot of time in the web design process?

V

Only during project kick off; your input and feedback are essential to your website design and development during the project scoping.

We will start with a discovery call about your needs, your favorites, your desire.

Then a scoping call to draft all your requirements.

We may also ask you to provide a sample website before starting the project. Remember, this is your site and we need your help and direction regarding your ultimate vision. We are always open to your ideas.

All the rest of the communication related to the project; such as, progress of completion, website assets, etc. will be via Slack.

Do you work with new and small businesses?

V

Yes! we cooperate with all types of companies - new, small and large companies. We are excited to be able to work and accompany new businesses that are passionate about taking their first steps in an online presence and revitalizing their vision.

Does my company need a website?

V

We often meet with business owners who feel they don't need a website, claiming that their business "was fine without it so far".

Although this may be true in some cases, so it definitely depends on your business model, but overall the need to be online is becoming less of a luxury and more necessity. Why?

Since Covid-19, more than 90% of consumers have shifted their shopping to be online before, If you don't adapt to our nowadays digital world, you may lose your competition game.
