Bloomreach

Bloomreach is the leader in digital Commerce Experience. It provides you with an upgrade to a CDP solution - CDXP (Customer Data and Experience Platform).Customers nowadays expect that they will receive a seamless customer experience. They are even willing to pay more for such an experience. But to provide a satisfying customer experience, you need to know your customers first. You need to familiarize yourself with their needs, preferences, and challenges and have that customer data ready to use, in real-time.
CDXP connects deep product data with deep customer data from various sources. This allows you to deliver incredible commerce experiences through personalized content and products across all digital touchpoints. This inevitably leads to true personalization, engagement, digital commerce growth, and higher revenue.

The portfolio of Bloomreach consists of 3 main products:

 brX - Bloomreach Experience Cloud
 brSM - Bloomreach Search & Merchandising
 brXM - Bloomreach Experience Manager

Bloomreach Experience Cloud (brX) is built to deliver B2B and B2C commerce experiences. It is the only solution that combines API-first ‘headless’ experience solution with personalization and optimization technology built on modern microservices architecture. Thanks to brX you can deliver relevant, contextual, and consistent customer experiences across all the touchpoints your customers interact with you (websites, apps, devices).

‍Bloomreach Search & Merchandising (brSM) offers a set of algorithms via API and advanced merchandising tools. These tools help your visitors to find products fast (by optimizing visitors’ search, browse, and landing page experiences). Machine Learning sets your site up for scale, while merchandisers can customize and improve results to perfect the product grid.

‍Bloomreach Experience Manager (brXM) is an open and flexible CMS designed for developers and marketers. The headless CMS is built to drive results fast – you can quickly create, test, and customize personalized digital experiences and integrate them with other systems.
Bloomreach serves hundreds of global brands including Bosch, Puma, Staples, Desigual, Marks & Spencer, B&S, Deutsche Telekom, Albertsons, FC Bayern München, NHS Digital, and many more.
