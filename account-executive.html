The Account Executive is responsible for achieving quota targets selling Minerva Visuals transformational CDP (cloud data platform) and MA (Marketing Automation) services to large web businesses. We are seeking proven high performers to over-achieve sales objectives and establish long-term relationships within assigned regions/named accounts.

Challenges

At Minerva Visuals, account executive’s primary responsibility is to support an organization’s client accounts. They’re generally the first or second person within an organization to handle the account, possibly after the front-line SDRs (Sales development representatives). They’re often responsible for closing deals once a lead has been qualified and nurtured by a SDR (Sales Development Representatives).Once they’ve made contact, it’s up to the account executive to manage that account by:

Building the relationship.
Identifying up-sell and cross-sell opportunities.
Producing/Handling SOWs (Scope of work) documents.
