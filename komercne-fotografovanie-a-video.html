Our Photography Process

We will meet at the chosen place, come to our studio or if it is a product, we will come pick it up from you.

When we meet, we will discuss the style that you need to implement in your photography session.

We are applying 'tethering technology' while shooting, so you will be able to see the photos live on a big 27" inch 5K screen  to be sure that you are getting the precise results you desire for your photos.

At the end of the photo shoot, we will send you a link to download all the high resolution , in addition to a c file with a the photos compressed for your e-commerce/eshop website Usually we provide the photos at the same days (up to 100 Images). If more 100, you will get your images within 48 hours.

For beauty products such as; cosmetics and make up, we also provide high-end retouch (Which can take up to 14 days) depends on the amount of photos.

Photography is Key

High quality studio photographs for your products is essential for increasing the value of your e-shop. The more professional your photo looks, the better your potential clients will look at you.
