Shopify

Shopify is the most well-known global commerce company. It provides an eCommerce platform that helps retail businesses of any size to start, grow, and manage their stores.
‍
Shopify is a complete commerce platform equipped with software that allows you to set up, design, and manage an online store and sell your products. With this platform, you can manage your products, inventory, payments, and shipping. It also contains a Marketing Platform, tools for analytics and reports, and much more.

This way you can deliver a better shopping experience for every customer.

Main system features:

‍Platform – Analytics, Performance, and Reliability.
‍
‍Merchandising/Marketing – Storefront Design Tools, Product Visualization, Recommendations, Personalization, Promotions, and Discounts.

‍Product Catalog – Content Management, Product Configuration, Product Search and Filtering.

‍Order Process – Checkout Process, Shipping Options, Tax Display and Processing, Payment Processing, Order Management.

Shopify is a cloud-based, multi-channel commerce platform. This multichannel approach allows merchants to manage their stores across multiple sales channels such as web, tablet and mobile, social media storefronts, marketplaces, brick-and-mortar locations, and pop-up shops.

Without a doubt, Shopify is a leader in the industry. The platform provides merchants with a single view of their business and customers. Shopify enables merchants to manage products and inventory, process orders and payments, build customer relationships, and improve personalization. 
‍
This platform has also an analytics tool that provides insights on total sales, conversion rates, average order value, returning customer rates, and a lot of other metrics valuable for online business.
‍
The Shopify platform was created for businesses of all sizes. It is currently used by more than 1,700,000 businesses in 175 countries around the world. Shopify is trusted by brands such as PepsiCo, Tesla, Red Bull, Gymshark, Allbirds, Fitbit, Heinz, and many more.
