Branding is our DNA

Marketing is like inviting someone on a date, branding is why they say "yes".

What to start with? One of the first things to consider when establishing a Brand is the design of the logo and visual identity.

This is always a challenging aspect, as it must embody the essence of your business, and create your most visually appealing Brand Image.

The logo is one of the main faces of your business and its design must meet the current and future needs of your brand.

This means thinking carefully about what it will look like on various media, such as online, mobile devices, banners, letterheads, invitations, business cards and much more.

From day one, you need to take design adaptability seriously to make sure you don't have to redesign and change the brand of your business.

In addition to logo design, there are many other visual elements of your company's brand that must achieve the right tone to achieve the highest possible recognition.

The style used on your website and how well it matches the design of other media is important.

The layout and design of all your media must be equally consistently high quality and must have a style that immediately evokes your brand.

When people read a brochure or catalog produced by your company, they must be able to pick it up on any page and know according to the design they are reading about.

Form and function need to be considered at an in-depth level to maintain the right balance. Ensuring that each platform is fully utilized so that your brand image stays in people's minds.

Branding in Bratislava is all around us and it is essential to know which things make a difference and how to create an excellent design.

At Minerva Visuals, we build brands for a variety of companies and understand the importance of cutting-edge branding and graphic design.

We accept the approach from the ground up and work in close contact with clients to understand their specific brand and the message they want to convey.

We take the time to listen to our clients, and that's what leads to the creative design process.

This allows us to create a stunning graphic design that speaks directly to the desired audience, while taking into account branding at every step. Contact us today and find out what the best branding agency in Bratislava can do to help you and your brand stand out from the crowd.

3 Tips for choosing the right Graphic Designer

Budget

Before you even start your search, determine how much you can spend on graphics. One thing to keep in mind is that like any other business investment, the more you invest in it, the higher the return.

Portfolio

Almost all Graphic Design companies will have a website representing their work. (And if not, mark it as a red flag.) Check out the online Graphic Design portfolio and review their previous work.

Your Story

Be prepared to talk about your company and its story. During the first discovery call, a good company will ask you to describe the personality, tone and future aspirations of your company.
