User.com

User.com is a marketing automation platform that helps businesses gain insights about customers, drive sales and help companies grow faster. This platform gathers all tools for marketing, sales, and support in one system. 
‍
Do you want to build personal and meaningful relationships with your customers?
‍
Software from User.com collects data about each customer individually in real-time. You can reach your customers through a wide range of communication channels: email campaigns, live chat, chatbot, push notifications, in-app messages, SMS, popups, dynamic page content, and many more. User.com integrates these communication channels with CRM, analytics, and reporting tools.
This platform allows you to automate your communication and internal processes, and manage sales easily. It also provides intuitive tools for the automation of marketing, sales, and customer support activities. Its main goal is to boost customer engagement, gain more leads, and improve conversions.
‍
With intuitive drag & drop tools, you can build marketing automation processes, marketing campaigns and create automated action paths that will continuously engage your customers. Use Triggers, Conditions, and Actions to deliver personalized messages to your users at the right time.
